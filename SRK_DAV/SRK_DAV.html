Principal's Message

Dear Students, Respected Teachers, Esteemed Parents, and Honorable Members of the Society,

 

As the Principal of this esteemed DAV institution, I feel both humbled and honored to be part of a legacy that is deeply rooted in the values of Maharishi Dayanand Saraswati ji and committed to academic excellence, moral integrity, and holistic development.
   
To My Dear Students:
You are the heart of this institution. Every day, I strive to create an environment where you feel inspired to dream, to question, to explore, and to grow—not only as brilliant minds but also as responsible, compassionate human beings. Remember, education is not just about marks or grades, but about becoming a person who can make a positive difference in the world.
 
To Our Teachers:
You are the guiding force behind every success story. I deeply value your dedication and hard work. Let us continue to work together to nurture curiosity, discipline, creativity, and resilience in our students. Let us be role models who teach not just through words, but by example.
 
To the Parents:
You are our most valuable partners in this journey. I thank you for your trust and support. Together, let us raise confident, cultured, and courageous children. I assure you that your children are in safe hands, where they are not only educated but also cared for and understood.
 
To the Society at Large:
Our school is not just an educational institution—it is a place where values are built, where future citizens are shaped. As a proud DAVian, I am committed to serving the society by nurturing individuals who are rooted in Indian culture, yet globally competent; who respect tradition, yet think innovatively.
 
 
 
In the true DAV spirit, I believe in continuous growth—not just of infrastructure or academics—but of hearts, minds, and character. Let us walk together in this journey of learning, progress, and service.
 
Warm regards,
MANISH KUMAR SINHA
